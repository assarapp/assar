¿Qué es?

Assar es una aplicación móvil en la cual tú puedes relizar tu hobby obteniendo una ganancia.
En donde los usuarios pueden encontrar cerca de ellos hobbies que quieran aprender y pagar por ellos, o pueden ofrecer su hobby a los demás para enseñar y así orecibir dinero.

  • Orientation

    Orientation is the gateway to campus, and also to Involvio. Students choose their orientation sessions, their track, and in under 30 seconds, their smartphone becomes a tool that helps them navigate their new environment.

  • Agenda

    Involvio knows the ins and outs of your students’ orientation schedules then seamlessly transitions to keep them organized as they get involved all year long. It even reminds them where they need to be and when.

  • Events

    On campus, and in the real world, events are social, so Involvio is too. Using their smartphones, your students can quickly comment on, share, see who's attending, and get directions to every event happening on campus.

  • Lists

    Encouraging continued and increased involvement is in our DNA (it's even in our name!). Involvio becomes your school's unified mobile calendar by integrating every existing calendar as a social list to which students can subscribe.

  • Updates

    Last minute change? Important message that needs immediate attention? Use this simple feature to send announcements to your students in real time as push notifications.

  • Explore

    Spontaneity is second nature to your students. Explore helps students find every event happening around their current location in real-time. It even shows events their friends are attending and helps new students find campus buildings.

¿Cómo funciona?

How does payment work?

Transparent hourly prices Once you post a task, you see hourly rates for the Taskers who are most qualified for your job. A minimum payment of one hour is required per task.
Secure, cash-free payments The entire payment process (including reimbursements) is handled securely online after a task is complete. There's no need for cash to change hands.
Service Fee TaskRabbit takes a service fee on each task so we can provide 24/7 Member Services support, full insurance on every task and our satisfaction guarantee.
Cancellation Policy You can cancel your task online or by calling our Member Services team. If you cancel within six hours of the start time agreed upon with your Tasker, you will be required to pay for one hour of work.
